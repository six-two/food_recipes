# Lumumba

A hot or cold chocolate drink with a shot of rum.

## Ingredients

- 150ml milk
- 40ml of rum
- 1 full tea spoon of cocoa powder
- sugar (optional)

### Tools

- Spoon
- Cup
- Some way of heating milk (optional, hot version)
- Fridge (optional, cold version)


## Directions

1. For the hot version: Heat milk and put it in the cup. For the cold version fill a cup with milk out of your fridge.
2. Mix the cocoa powder (and sugar) into the milk and stir it for a bit
3. Add the rum
