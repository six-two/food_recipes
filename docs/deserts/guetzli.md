# Guetzeli

They taste a lot like Butterspekulatius, not sure if it is intended.

## Ingredients

- 250g flour
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 150g sugar
- 150g butter
- 1 egg

This resulted in two sheets full of cookies.

## Tools

- Oven
- Fridge
- Baking sheet(s) & Baking sheet paper
- Baking roll
- Scale
- Plastic wrap
- Cookie Cutter (alternatively a knife for cutting out rectangles)

## Notes

- Original recipe (Swiss, written in German): [1 Teig, 5 Guetzli](https://www.swissmilk.ch/de/rezepte-kochideen/rezepte/SM2021_FAMI_01/1-teig-5-guetzli/)
- Dough is very sticky when warm, so you want to cool it for about the time I estimated.
    If you cool it too long, it gets very hard to roll out.
    Though you could probably roll it out mostly before you put it in the fridge.
